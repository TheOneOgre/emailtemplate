Monitors

LG UltraGear Monitor 24" 1080p IPS 144hz FreeSync HDR10

Probably the best bang for the buck display you can have at this size and price. The most important features are IPS for color accuracy and viewing angles, 144hz for gaming and better viewing, and the borderless frame makes the look that much more premium. For 24 inch, this is what I could recommend, if you can go up to 27" you can open yourself up to more options, let me know!

1080p is all that would really be necessary at 24", the Pixel density is high enough moving up to 1440p wouldn't make a big difference visually but the cost and GPU power would go up, resulting in lower FPS. Stick with 1080p at 24"

$149 new, Amazon Warehouse has it for $112 if you wanna try that
Purchase on Amazon

Microphones

Audio-Technica AT2005USB Cardioid Dynamic USB/XLR

This is a great cardioid microphone. Cardioids are great for noisy environments as they only pickup sound right near them. This also means you will need to have it very close to you for proper pickup. Depends on your environment and what you want. 

Cardioid is best for noisy environments, condenser is better for quiet or if you like picking up the whole room

$59 on Amazon, I have one New in box never opened for $50 if you want it. Purchase this and the headphones for $90 and I'll throw in a pop filter for this.
Purchase on Amazon

Blue Yeti USB Microphone

It's big, it's black, it's... the most common microphone among gamers since like 2010 lol. This is my daily microphone and it has served me extremely well.
